### Description

HomeMakers brings early home planning, professional discovery, and project tracking into one place.

Create a new-build or remodel brief, use AI to explore concepts and indicative estimates, and save the result to your account. Return later from any device to continue the same project.

Homeowners can:

- Create and save new-home and remodel projects
- Generate early AI concepts, plans, and indicative estimates
- Track stage progress from completed checklist tasks
- Add project tasks and discussion updates
- Save private documents, team contacts, and payment records
- Discover professionals from their published portfolios

Professionals can:

- Build a portfolio with business details and work photos
- Submit a public profile for review; approved profiles appear in discovery
- Share a portfolio link with potential clients

AI concepts and estimates are preliminary planning aids, not sanction drawings, structural advice, or fixed quotations. Always validate designs, compliance, and costs with qualified professionals.

## Review notes draft

HomeMakers supports homeowner and professional roles. Email/password sign-up is available in the app. Public discovery and public professional profiles can be reviewed without an account. Project, document, team, payment-ledger, and portfolio-editing features require authentication.

The payment ledger records owner-entered project payments; it does not transfer money. The native app does not present Razorpay checkout or an external purchase link.

Provide App Review with a dedicated homeowner test account and a professional test account before submission. Do not place credentials in this repository.
