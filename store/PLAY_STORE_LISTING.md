## Full description

Plan, organize, and continue your home project from one account.

HomeMakers helps homeowners create a new-build or remodel brief, explore early AI concepts and indicative estimates, discover published professionals, and manage saved project work with checklists.

HOMEOWNER TOOLS

• Create and save new-home or remodel projects
• Generate early AI concepts and indicative estimates
• Track stage progress automatically from completed tasks
• Add tasks and project discussion updates
• Save private documents, team contacts, and payment records
• Return from another device and continue the same project

PROFESSIONAL TOOLS

• Create a professional portfolio
• Add business information and work photos
• Submit a professional profile for review before it appears in discovery
• Share a public portfolio link

AI concepts, plans, and estimates are preliminary planning aids. They are not sanction drawings, structural advice, or guaranteed quotations. Validate all construction decisions with qualified architects, engineers, and contractors.

The in-project payment ledger records information entered by the homeowner and does not itself transfer money.
