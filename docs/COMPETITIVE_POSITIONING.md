# Competitive positioning — pitch reference

*Internal note for founders, investors, and partners. Use in decks when someone says “you’re like Material Depot / Room Story / Livspace.” **We are not.** Update when a competitor ships something new.*

---

## One-line HomeMakers

**AI-powered project OS for homeowners who keep control:** vision → v0 concepts & indicative estimate → **post for pro quotes** (or bring your own team) → **project management** through build — with an **open contractor & architect marketplace**, not turn-key delivery or a materials catalog.

---

## Quick comparison

| Player | What they really sell | AI role | HomeMakers overlap? |
|--------|------------------------|---------|---------------------|
| **Material Depot** | Materials commerce + stores + inspiration + 3D visualizer | Merchandising, discovery, shop-the-look | **Low** — we don’t lead with SKU checkout |
| **Room Story AI** (and similar) | “Found a photo → AI finds products → buy” | Visual **product matching** & purchase | **Low** — we don’t optimize for item cart |
| **Livspace / Homelane** | **End-to-end** design + delivery (their network, their PM) | Planning inside *their* managed journey | **Different category** — managed interior, not open market |
| **HomeMakers** | Brief + v0 + **quotes from pros** + execution hub | **Redesign / concepts / estimate** on *your* project; pros quote real scope | **This is us** |

## Material Depot — materials retailer, not project OS

**Their capability**

- Massive **SKU catalog** (tiles, laminates, wallpaper, louvers, quartz, panels).
- **Pincode delivery**, experience centres, video-call shopping.
- **Inspiration** galleries, room-based browsing, shop-this-look, 3D visualizer.
- Tools: cost calculator, EMI, buying guides — optimised for **buying finishes**.

**Our capability (different job)**

- **Contractor & architect marketplace** — homeowners post projects and get **bids / proposals / quotes**, or invite pros they already know.
- **AI-powered redesign** — new-home & remodel wizards, **v0** concept pack + **indicative estimate** from the brief (not “match this Pinterest tile SKU”).
- **Project management hub** — phases, tasks, documents, team, site feed (execution after you hire).

**Pitch line**

> “Material Depot helps you **buy materials**. HomeMakers helps you **run the project** — AI clarity first, then **open-market quotes** from architects and contractors, then one hub until keys in hand. We might link to material partners later; we are **not** a depot homepage.”

---

## Room Story AI (and “visual search → cart” players)

**Their model**

- User uploads or finds an **inspiration image**.
- AI **identifies products** in the scene and routes to **purchase** (tiles, furniture, decor).
- Value = **shopping conversion** and product affiliate / retail margin.

**Our model**

- User describes **space, goals, budget, constraints** (plus photos in remodel flow).
- AI produces a **project brief + v0 visuals + ballpark estimate** for **scope discussion**.
- Next step = **professionals quote** design, sanction, and build work — not an automated shopping list from one image.

**Pitch line**

> “Room Story–style products answer: *‘What should I buy from this picture?’* HomeMakers answers: *‘What’s my project, what might it cost, and **who should I hire** to do the next phase?’* Totally different wedge.”

---

## Livspace / Homelane — managed end-to-end, not open marketplace

**Their model**

- **Own** (or tightly control) design → procurement → execution.
- Homeowner buys a **package** inside **their** network and PM.
- Great for the **~5–10%** who want “do it for me” turn-key interiors.
- AI (where used) serves **their** delivery funnel, not an neutral OS.

**Our model**

- Homeowner stays **in control** — independent architects, contractors, local builders (**~82–90%** of the market per our research framing).
- **Post project** for marketplace quotes **or bring your own team**.
- We don’t replace the pro; we give **one brief, one v0 pack, one project room** so quotes and site work aren’t scattered on WhatsApp.

**Pitch line**

> “Livspace and Homelane are **operators** — they sell you *their* end-to-end delivery. HomeMakers is **infrastructure** — AI + marketplace + PM for people who **choose their own pros**. Comparing us to them is like comparing Stripe to a bank branch.”

## Where we might partner (not compete)

| Partner type | Relationship |
|--------------|--------------|
| **Material Depot–class retailers** | Future: curated **materials** from an approved BOM / selections tab — homeowner still runs project on HomeMakers. |
| **Room Story–class visual commerce** | Optional inspiration import; we don’t become a product-matching checkout. |
| **Livspace / Homelane** | Different ICP; some users may start managed and later want control — rarely direct swap. |

---

## Slide-ready “we are / we are not”

**We are**

- AI **v0** for new build & remodel (concepts + indicative estimate).
- **Open marketplace** for architects, contractors, trades.
- **Post project → get quotes** or **bring your own professionals**.
- **Project management** after hire (tasks, phases, docs, team).

**We are not**

- A **materials e-commerce** homepage (Material Depot).
- An **image → product → buy** engine (Room Story AI).
- A **turn-key interior** operator (Livspace / Homelane).
